Doctor Appointment Booking System
1. Introduction
In many hospitals and clinics, patients often face difficulties while booking appointments with
doctors. Traditional appointment booking methods usually involve phone calls, manual
registers, or waiting in long queues. These methods are time-consuming and may lead to
appointment conflicts, missed schedules, and patient dissatisfaction.
Hospitals and clinics require a centralized platform where patients can easily view doctor
availability, book appointments, and receive reminders. Such a system can reduce waiting
time, improve communication between doctors and patients, and help hospitals manage
schedules more effectively.
The Doctor Appointment Booking System is designed to provide a digital solution for
appointment scheduling. Patients can search for doctors, select available consultation slots,
and receive notifications. Doctors can manage their schedules, while administrators can
monitor appointment records and hospital activities.


2. Description
The Doctor Appointment Booking System is a web-based application developed using the
MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js). The system provides separate
dashboards for patients, doctors, and administrators.
Patients can register, log in, search for doctors by specialization, hospital, location, or
availability, and book appointments according to suitable time slots. After booking, patients
receive appointment confirmation messages and reminders before the scheduled
consultation. Patients can also view their appointment history, cancel appointments, or
reschedule if required.
Doctors can log in to their dashboard and manage their available consultation timings. They
can view patient details, appointment schedules, and update their availability status. Doctors
can also maintain consultation records and notes for future reference.
Administrators are responsible for managing doctor profiles, patient accounts, appointment
records, and hospital details. They can monitor the overall functioning of the system,
approve doctor registrations, and resolve appointment conflicts.
The system improves efficiency, reduces patient waiting time, and ensures better
communication between hospitals, doctors, and patients.

4. Technical Architecture
The Doctor Appointment Booking System consists of the following components:
в—Џ Frontend Application: Built using React.js for responsive and user-friendly
interfaces.
в—Џ Backend Server: Built using Node.js and Express.js for handling APIs and business
logic.
в—Џ Authentication Module: Manages secure login, registration, and role-based access.
в—Џ Doctor Module: Handles doctor profiles, specialization, schedules, and availability.
в—Џ Patient Module: Allows patients to search doctors, book appointments, and manage
records.
в—Џ Appointment Module: Manages booking, rescheduling, cancellation, and
appointment tracking.
в—Џ Notification Module: Sends confirmation and reminder messages through email or
SMS.
в—Џ Database: MongoDB stores doctor, patient, appointment, and hospital records.
в—Џ Admin Dashboard: Helps administrators manage users, schedules, and
appointment history.


5. ER-Diagram & Relationships
The important relationships in the Doctor Appointment Booking System are:
в—Џ Patient-Appointment Relationship: One patient can book multiple appointments.
в—Џ Doctor-Appointment Relationship: One doctor can have multiple appointments.
в—Џ Doctor-Specialization Relationship: One specialization can belong to multiple
doctors.
в—Џ Appointment-Notification Relationship: One appointment can generate multiple
notifications.
в—Џ Admin-Doctor Relationship: One administrator can manage multiple doctor
profiles.
в—Џ Doctor-Hospital Relationship: One hospital can have multiple doctors.
These relationships help maintain proper records of appointments, doctors, and patients.
